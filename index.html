Frequently Asked Questions

What languages do you offer translation services for?

We provide translation services for a wide range of languages, including major global languages like English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Arabic, and many more.

What types of documents do you translate?

We translate legal documents, medical reports, business contracts, technical manuals, academic papers, websites, and more.

How do you ensure the accuracy of translations?

Our translators are native speakers with expertise in various fields, and we follow a rigorous quality control process, including proofreading and peer reviews.

How much does your translation service cost?

Pricing depends on factors like language pair, document type, word count, and urgency. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, we follow strict confidentiality policies and can sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) upon request to protect your sensitive information.

Do you offer revisions if I am not satisfied with the translation?

Yes, we offer free revisions within a specified period to ensure complete client satisfaction.
